CEREAL BOX PROJECT

"Newton's Apples"

Newton's Apples - Cereal Box by nintendoto on Sketchfab

ASSIGNMENT: Design a cereal box that showcases a given historical figure.

     For my end of course final project for AP World History, I was assigned to create a cereal box featuring Isaac Newton. I played off of his common myth that Isaac Newton was inspired to discover gravity upon witnessing an apple falling from a tree by parodying the design of a box of Apple Jacks cereal. In order to accomplish maximum authenticity and quality, I scanned each side of the original cereal box to emulate, drawing over each face in Paint.NET.
