LIGHT SHOW PROJECT

"The Relationship"

ASSIGNMENT: Put together a music mash-up with a theme, and create a light show to go with it.

     For my light show, I decided to take the theme prompt a step further and use the music mash-up to tell a story. The songs progress as follows:

 

"I Need Your Love" - Ellie Goulding

"Blank Space" - Taylor Swift

"XO" - Beyonc√©

"Where'd You Go?" - Fort Minor

 

     The songs in conjunction tell the story of a relationship- from the desire that comes upon first sight, flourishing into full admiration, and ending tragically. I wanted my presentation to evoke emotion, which is why I chose such a strong theme and twist.

     The 4 LED light fixtures used were daisy-chained to a Chauvet Obey 70 LED Board. In order to get a grasp of the board's full potential, I briefed myself by going over the board's manual. There, I discovered that the board had MIDI functionality, a very useful feature I capitalized upon to get my light cues timed just right.

 

     A special component to the entire project was my use of the Garritan Virtual Instrument Sound Library, specifically the Steinway Grand Piano, Glockenspiel, and Pizzicato Bass from Garritan Personal Orchestra 4, to accompany the vocals of the songs. Doing so gave me full control of the accompaniment in order to facilitate the transitions to other songs, such as chord changes, leading to a much more cohesive mash-up.
