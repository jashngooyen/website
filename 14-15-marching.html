"Wild Blue Yonder"

     "Wild Blue Yonder" is the second show I've marched, and the first show I've marched as the clarinet section leader. On the field as Clarinet 3, I served as a role-model marcher for the new style of marching we adopted: straight-leg marching. Building off of my leadership skills from managing an online community, I continued building my leadership skills face-to-face in a flexible environment.

     The performance shown here is from the last competition of the season, the Wylie Marching Invitational. The show itself goes through the life of two life-long friends that enlist in the Air Force together. Pieces featured in order are:
